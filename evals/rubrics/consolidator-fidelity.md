# Rubric: consolidation fidelity (anchored 1-4)

You are scoring the CANDIDATE — a consolidator's merged result — for fidelity
to its inputs. Judge only the content in front of you. Do not reward length.

- **4 — faithful and vigilant.** Carries forward every caveat/assumption/
  key decision from the inputs with sources; flags disagreements between
  workers instead of papering over them; missing or unverified artifacts are
  called out explicitly.
- **3 — faithful with small losses.** The merge is accurate but drops a minor
  caveat or attributes something to the wrong source; artifact verification
  happened but is reported thinly.
- **2 — lossy.** Material caveats or a disagreement between inputs vanished
  in the merge, or claimed artifacts were accepted without verification.
- **1 — unfaithful.** The merged result contradicts its inputs, invents
  consensus where there was disagreement, or treats missing artifacts as
  present.
